Here is a sample leave application:

**Application for Leave**

To,
[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Designation]
[Date]

Respected [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to request a leave of [number of days] days from [start date] to [end date]. I need to take this leave due to [briefly mention the reason for leave, e.g. personal reasons, family emergency, illness, etc.].

I will ensure that all my tasks are completed and my work is up to date before taking the leave. I will also be available by email if anything urgent comes up while I am away.

I would like to request your approval for my leave application. If there are any issues or concerns, please let me know and I will do my best to resolve them.

Thank you for considering my application.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
[Your Designation]
[Your Contact Information]

Please note that this is just a sample and you should customize it according to your needs and the company's policies.
